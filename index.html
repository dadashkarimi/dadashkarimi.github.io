Biography

    Hi, I am Javid Dadashkarimi. Thank you for visiting my website! I am a researcher at the Martinos Center at MGH/Harvard, under the supervision of Prof. Malte Hoffmann and Prof. Bruce Fischl on the applications of artificial intelligence (AI) in fetal brain imaging. I earned my Ph.D. from the Computer Science Department at Yale University, primarily under the supervision of Prof. Dustin Scheinost. I completed both my BSc and MSc degrees at the University of Tehran. Currently, I am working on developing AI models for fetal brain imaging. In my spare time, I enjoy photography, watching football, and playing tennis when I'm not doing science : )
