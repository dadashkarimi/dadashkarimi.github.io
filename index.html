IID Group

  • Inference, Information, and Decision Systems Group

    Welcome to the I.I.D. group, led by Amin Karbasi. The research in our group is at the intersection of learning theory[1], optimization[2,3], and information processing[4,5,6]. We develop data-driven algorithms, with strong theoretical guarantees, that can automatically acquire and reason about highly uncertain information. For more information, please visit our research directions.
