IID Group

Inference, Information, and Decision Systems Group

  • The research in (I.I.D.) Systems Group at Yale University is at the intersection of learning theory, optimization, and information processing. We develop data-driven algorithms, with strong theoretical guarantees, that can automatically acquire and reason about highly uncertain information. Our application domains include interactive recommender systems, neuroscience, and optimal robust data gathering.
